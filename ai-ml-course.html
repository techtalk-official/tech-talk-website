What is AI? The Real Story
Cut through the hype — understand what AI truly is, what it can and can't do, and why it matters for your career right now.

AI has been "around the corner" for 70 years. Alan Turing asked "Can machines think?" back in 1950. So why does it suddenly feel like AI just arrived?

The Myth vs The Reality

Most people think AI means robots with consciousness — like in movies. The reality: AI is software that finds patterns in data and uses those patterns to make predictions or generate content. That's it.

A Quick Timeline

  • 1950s — Turing Test proposed
  • 1960s-1980s — "AI Winters": big promises, small results
  • 2012 — Deep Learning breakthrough (ImageNet)
  • 2022 — ChatGPT launches, AI goes mainstream

Three Types of AI

  • Narrow AI — exists today. Good at ONE task (e.g., ChatGPT)
  • General AI (AGI) — human-level across all tasks. Doesn't exist yet
  • Super AI — beyond human. Purely theoretical

Key Takeaway

AI is a tool, not magic. Understanding what it can and can't do is your superpower.
